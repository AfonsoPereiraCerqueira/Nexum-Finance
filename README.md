# Nexum Finance 💰
> A modern, intuitive, and secure solution for personal and business financial management.

[![PRs Welcome](https://img.shields.io/badge/PRs-welcome-blue.svg)](https://github.com/AfonsoPereiraCerqueira/Nexum-Finance/pulls)

Nexum Finance is a comprehensive financial tracking application designed to bridge the gap between complex accounting and daily expense management. It empowers users to take full control of their financial health through data-driven insights and a seamless user experience.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

* **Real-time Dashboard:** A high-level overview of your net worth, monthly spending, and budget status.
* **Transaction Management:** Easily log, categorize, and search through your income and expenses.
* **Dynamic Analytics:** Visual charts and reports to understand where your money is going.
* **Budget Goal Setting:** Set monthly limits for specific categories and receive alerts.
* **Data Security:** End-to-end encryption for your sensitive financial data.
* **Multi-currency Support:** Manage accounts in different currencies with automated exchange rate updates.
